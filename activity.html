AI Sign Language Project Exhibition (SignLingo)

Excited to showcase our project, SignLingo...

Excited to showcase our project, SignLingo, during a technology exhibition. SignLingo is an AI-powered sign language learning platform designed to help users learn sign language through interactive lessons, quizzes, and gesture recognition. During the exhibition, we demonstrated how the system works and explained how AI can be used to recognize hand gestures and support accessible learning. It was a great experience presenting the project and discussing the technology behind it with visitors.
